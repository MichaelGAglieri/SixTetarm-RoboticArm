COLLABORATIVE ROBOTIC ARM EB-15Introducing the EB-15 Collaborative Robotic Arm – 
a cutting-edge addition to the world of robotics. With its sleek design and lightweight, 
this 3D printable STL model works with just 6 NEMA17 motors which use EBA-17 and EBA-17-S planetary gear actuators which endow it with strong holding capabilities. 
It is articulated with 6 axes of movement. Perfect for robotics enthusiasts and students.For the robotic arm model in STEP file https://toolboxrobotics.com/shop
